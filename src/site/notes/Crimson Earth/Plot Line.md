# **0. Design Constraint
## Advancement Rule

Players advance when they achieve **Milestones + Acting + Exposure**

|**Requirement**|**Needed**|
|---|---|
|Milestone (story)|mandatory|
|Acting alignment|reduces risk|
|Essence acquisition|fuels upgrade|

---

# **1. Starting Sequences (Session 1)**

Players begin at:
**Pseudo-Sequence 9 (Unstable State)**

They are:

- not fully Beyonders
- partially fused with the **factory object**

---
## **Mechanic:**

**Fragmented Essence**

Each player starts with:

- 1 passive ability
- 1 unstable ability (roll for side effects)

---

# **2. Recommended Party Composition (3–4 players)**

Keep diversity in **function**, not just combat.

---

## **Player Pathways

### **1. Sleepless (Control / Dream)**
- Dream intrusion
- Anti-corruption support

### **2. Seer (Utility / Prediction)**
- Advantage manipulation
- Info gathering

### **3. Tyrant (Combat)**
- Tracking
- burst damage

### **4. Cultist / Occultist (Risk player)**
- high power
- corruption driver

### 5. Red Priest (Tracking / Trapping)
- MT
- fire
---

# **3. Sequence Progression (Accelerated)**

|**Session**|**Sequence**|**Description**|
|---|---|---|
|S1|9 (unstable)|partial awakening|
|S2|8|controlled abilities|
|S3|7|identity stabilisation|
|S4|6|real Beyonder|
|S5|5|influence level|
|S6|4|elite / mini-boss tier|

---

## **Key Rule**

Advancement requires **essence + acting + narrative trigger**

---

# **4. Core Plot Arc (6 Sessions)**

---

# **SESSION 1 —**

**Factory Incident**

### **Theme: Survival + Confusion**

### **Key events:**

- wake in new bodies
- discover dead factory workers
- first exposure to object
- police arrive

---

### **Gameplay:**

- investigation skill checks
- first combat: **minor corrupted workers**
- dream sequence (1 player at a time)

---

### **Boss:**

**“Half-Manifested Limb Entity”**

- cannot be killed normally
- must:
    - break line of sight
    - or trigger instability

---

### **Reward:**

→ Sequence 8 unlock

---

# **SESSION 2 —**

**Police / Bureau Intake**

### **Theme: Control + Suspicion**

- interrogation (roleplay heavy)
- dream intrusion test (bureau test)
- introduction to factions

---

### **Side mission:**

- small anomaly cleanup

---

### **Boss:**

**Mirror Fragment**

- attacks through reflections
- introduces:  
    → perception mechanics

---

### **Reward:**

→ Sequence 7

---

# **SESSION 3 —**

**Return to Factory**

### **Theme: Investigation → Truth**

- explore underground cult site
- discover:
    - ritual circle
    - missing workers used as sacrifices

---

### **Mechanic:**

- corruption increases faster here

---

### **Boss:**

**Cult Overseer (Sequence 7 equivalent)**

- partially possessed
- phase shift:
    - physical → dream layer

---

### **Reward:**

→ Sequence 6

---
# **SESSION 4 —**

**Cult Expansion**

### **Theme: Pressure**

- multiple incidents across city
- choose:
    - follow orders
    - investigate deeper

---

### **Key reveal:**

Object is a **fragment of Outside God ritual anchor**

---

### **Boss:**

**Dual-layer enemy (important)**

- must be fought in:
    - real world
    - dream world simultaneously

---

### **Reward:**

→ Sequence 5

---
# **SESSION 5 —**

**Corruption Arc**

### **Theme: Temptation**

Players encounter:

- cultists offering power
- shortcuts to Sequence 4

---

### **Mechanic:**

- optional fast advancement with corruption penalty

---

### **Boss:**

**Fellow “survivor” from factory**

- chose cult path
- now stronger than players

---

### **Reward:**

→ Sequence 4

---

# **SESSION 6 —**

**Anchor Event**

### **Theme: Resolution**

### **Final dungeon:**

- ritual site beneath city

---

### **Final Boss:**

**Ritual Anchor Entity**

Multi-phase:

1. Physical form
2. Dream manifestation
3. Identity overwrite attempt

---

### **Win conditions:**

- destroy anchor
- seal it
- or take control (dark ending)

---

# **5. Sequence Ability Design (Example)**

---

## **Sequence 9 → 8**

### **Sleepless**

- Dream peek (see hidden info)
- Passive: reduced corruption

---

### **Seer**

- Minor prediction (reroll once)
- Passive: detect anomalies

---

### **Hunter**

- Tracking mark
- Passive: bonus damage
---

## **Sequence 7–6**

### **Sleepless**

- Enter dreams physically
- Memory alteration

---

### **Seer**

- Future glimpse (advantage system)
- Illusion projection

---

### **Hunter**

- Multi-target burst
- instinct dodge
---

## **Sequence 5–4**

### **Sleepless**

- Force enemies into dream state
- large area control

---

### **Seer**

- fate manipulation (limited)
- enemy misfire

---

### **Tyrant**

- boss-level DPS
- anti-Beyonder damage

---

# **6. Core Mechanics**

**Wrong Solution = Death**

Players must learn:

|**Situation**|**Correct Approach**|
|---|---|
|mirror enemy|break perception|
|dream entity|fight mentally|
|cult boss|disrupt ritual|

---

# **7. XP / Progression System**

Do NOT reward only combat.

---

## **Advancement Triggers**

|**Action**|**Reward**|
|---|---|
|solving anomaly correctly|high XP|
|acting alignment|corruption reduction|
|killing blindly|corruption ↑|

---

# **8. Party Dynamics (Important)**

Encourage:

- information sharing
- role specialisation
- disagreement (moral tension)
